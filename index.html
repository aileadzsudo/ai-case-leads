Questions Answered

Everything your firm needs to know before partnering with Ai Case Leads.

A lead is a name and phone number — someone who may or may not qualify and hasn't agreed to anything. A case, as we define it, is a prospect who has been fully vetted against your firm's criteria, reviewed your retainer agreement, and signed it. When we deliver a case, the work of converting a prospect into a client is already done. You receive a client, not a contact.
Yes — unconditionally. Every case is generated and delivered exclusively for your firm. We never sell, share, or re-broker a case to any other law firm. Once a case is assigned to your firm, it is yours and only yours. We don't run a marketplace. We don't recycle inventory.
Our entire pipeline is in-house, built around compliance from the ground up. We run our own ads, operate our own landing pages, and employ our own call center. When a prospect opts in, they consent directly — creating a clean, documented record. There are no third-party vendors involved, which eliminates the common sources of TCPA exposure that plague other providers.
After each case is delivered, your intake team has 7 days to review and confirm it meets the agreed criteria. If a case falls short — wrong type, outside your geography, or doesn't meet injury standards — notify us and we'll issue a replacement at no additional cost. No disputes, no delays.
Our media buying team builds and runs targeted campaigns across TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube using proprietary AI to identify recently injured individuals. Those people land on our pre-vetting page, opt in, and consent to be contacted. Our in-house call center then qualifies and signs them to your retainer. Everything is owned and operated by us.
At onboarding, we build a custom qualification profile based on your exact intake standards — case types, states, injury severity, accident recency, insurance requirements, and any other criteria your team uses. Our call center applies your standards to every prospect, not a generic template.
Auto accidents represent the majority of our volume. We also generate truck and commercial vehicle, rideshare, slip and fall, motorcycle, and pedestrian accident cases. Volume and case type targeting can be configured around your firm's priorities.
We operate nationwide. We know your firm is licensed to practice in specific states, and we configure all case delivery to match your licensed jurisdictions. Cases are only signed to your retainer within states you're authorized to practice in. Multi-state firms are fully supported.
After an initial conversation where we align on intake criteria, case types, and volume targets, we configure your qualification profile and begin generating campaigns. Onboarding is straightforward — fill out the contact form and our team will walk you through the rest.

The prospect has been identified, contacted, qualified, and signed before they reach your team. Our in-house model means no third-party TCPA risk. Our AI reaches injured people before competitors.
