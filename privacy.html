1 Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly when inquiring about our services, including:

  • Name and title
  • Law firm name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • States where your firm is licensed to practice
  • Monthly case volume goals or budget information you submit through our forms

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, for security and site performance purposes.

2 How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Review and process service inquiries and partnership requests
  • Coordinate and deliver signed retainer agreement acquisition services
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send service-related communications by phone, email, or SMS when applicable and with consent
  • Maintain compliance records (including opt-in/opt-out records where required)
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Improve our website and services over time

NextGen Distribution LLC dba Ai Case Leads is a case acquisition company serving law firms. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice or representation.

3 SMS Consent and Mobile Data

If you choose to provide a mobile phone number and opt in to receive text messages from Ai Case Leads (NextGen Distribution LLC dba Ai Case Leads), you may receive SMS messages related to service updates, partnership coordination, follow-ups regarding submitted information, and general client communications. Providing your phone number and consenting to SMS is not a condition of any purchase or service. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Text HELP to (661) 860-2032 for assistance. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Upon sending STOP, we will confirm your unsubscribe status via SMS. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. For questions about your text plan or data plan, contact your wireless provider.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support of services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent — this information will not be shared with any third parties. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except for aggregators and providers of the text message services.

4 Information Sharing

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers: We may share information with subcontractors or vendors who provide support services (such as customer service, software tools, hosting, analytics, or communications) solely to perform services on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell your personal information.

5 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and improve our services.

  • Essential Cookies: Used to ensure the website functions properly and securely.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website, including pages viewed and time spent on pages.
  • Advertising / Tracking Tools: We may use tools such as Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, TikTok Pixel, or similar technologies to measure advertising performance and improve marketing campaigns.

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6 Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

7 Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain information (subject to legal and compliance requirements)
  • Adjust cookie settings in your browser
  • Unsubscribe from email communications using the unsubscribe link in any email we send
  • Contact us directly with questions or requests about your information

8 Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain compliance records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal obligations. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to securely delete or anonymize it.

9 Children's Privacy

Our services are intended exclusively for licensed attorneys and law firm representatives who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our website, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of how we are protecting your information.
